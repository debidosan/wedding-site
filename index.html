The Sacred Ceremony

A morning of tradition at Akasaka Hikawa Shrine

Akasaka Hikawa Shrine

Built in 1730, this historic sanctuary is dedicated to the deities of marriage. We invite you to witness our vows amidst the serene greenery of Akasaka.

Time: 11:00 AM Ceremony
(Please arrive by 10:30 AM for registration)

Address: 6-10-12 Akasaka, Minato-ku, Tokyo

Shinto Traditions

Sanshin: The solemn procession of the couple led by priests and Gagaku musicians into the sacred main hall.
Sankon no gi: The exchange of nuptial sake cups, symbolizing the formal, lifelong bond between bride and groom.
Gagaku: Ancient Japanese court music performed live by musicians to accompany the sacred rites.
